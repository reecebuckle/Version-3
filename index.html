üõ∞Ô∏è About This Project

This visualisation suite was developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025, specifically targeting the Terra's 25th Anniversary Animation Challenge. The project showcases 23 years of ocean chlorophyll-a data from NASA's MODIS Aqua satellite, demonstrating seasonal patterns, climate trends, and ocean productivity cycles.

Data Source: NASA Ocean Color Web - MODIS Aqua Level-3 Chlorophyll-a (4km resolution)
Time Range: 2002-2025 (Monthly averages)
Technology: WebGL Globe, Three.js, Custom GLSL Shaders
Purpose: Educational visualisation and scientific communication
